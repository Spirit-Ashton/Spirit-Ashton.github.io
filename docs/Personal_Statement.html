I'm Spirit-Ashton.

What Started it all?
While I always had an affinity for all things computing, at 10 I had found scratch- a block-code-based programming website. I was enamoured and had made games for me and my brother to play, this is what started my interest in developing!
How did I get here?
After making many games I decided to try and use a proper game engine for once, apart from leading me to learn about game design It opened me to the world of pixel art! From there I set myself to broaden my creative abilities and tried my hand at graphic design, photo and video editing and logo design. These experiences have led me here today- with this website to show off my work!
Why are these experiences important to me?
When learning about all these facets of design, I found that what interested me the most was how important the design choices made impacted the psychology of the user, and how focused each designer was on being able to manipulate this to create the best possible experience. Having all these areas seemingly converge to this one central idea makes me want to learn more and use these ideas to be able to develop amazingly experiences also.
What is my goal?
I aim to develop all my skills to a point where they can all stand-alone at professional level quality. To be able to make strong functional systems or experiences and support them with quality design focused on fine-tuning the experience to a high standard on the psychological level.
