Insíonne

• Lua Based Platformer
Insíonne is my ambitious A-Level Project. I used this to teach myself Defold and Lua aswell as experiment with what I could develop.
Long Development
The most valuable aspect of this project was that it required a controlled, long-term development cycle. This allowed me to develop a strong Design and Plan beforehand.
Aims
With Inspiration from platformer games such as Sonic Mania and Celeste, I had aimed to create a fast paced technical platformer game.
A deep dive into learning how to design and follow the workflow of software development.
Custom Camera
The Camera Reacts to the speed of the player zooming out to see more whilst also moving forward to give extra lookahead. The Camera can also be adjusted up or down manually.
Wall Jumps and Air Boosts
The main gimmick of this Game is the movement, being developed for controller you are given an Air Boost in the joystick direction as well as quick consecutive wall jumps!
Parallax Background
The project features a parallaxed infinitely scrolling background with 11 layers. Developing this required pixel perfect measurements!
Insíonne gave me a very strong understanding of how to solve logic problems specific to game-dev.
Pebbler!
I Designed and animated the Enemy Sprites using Aseprite, keeping in mind the colliders that would have to be applied.
Persistent Enemies
I created these small enemies for the project- endlessly chasing the player and intermittently attacking.
Alongside this project I have written a Design Analysis Document, detailing my process and understanding.
